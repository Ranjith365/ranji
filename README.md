<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Courier New, Courier, monospace; font-size: x-large;">ARE YOU READY</span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Courier New, Courier, monospace; font-size: x-large;"><a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-BrL5LLMqB_Q/XUPVA8gmz0I/AAAAAAAAAEA/ImWTyar1OTcUihYskJ6JwvGi5xgwyzcbwCEwYBhgL/s1600/images9.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="225" data-original-width="225" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-BrL5LLMqB_Q/XUPVA8gmz0I/AAAAAAAAAEA/ImWTyar1OTcUihYskJ6JwvGi5xgwyzcbwCEwYBhgL/s1600/images9.jpg" /></a></span></div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="424d" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em; text-align: start;">
<span style="font-family: Courier New, Courier, monospace; font-size: x-large;">Network Marketing has gained a lot of importance in India in the last decade. This is the platform that combines flexibility of a business and paycheck system of job, to give you best of both worlds. Today in this post, I will walk you through each step required to start and build a successful network marketing business on your own. Grab a pen and a paper and let us get started!</span></div>
<br />
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="c3a8" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
Once you have decided which network marketing company to join and how to sign up with them, follow the steps below to kick-start your business.</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="3a3f" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
<span class="it jf" style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: 700;">STEP 1 : KNOW YOUR WHY.</span></div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="aec0" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
To be successful in any business, one must first have a dream. Your first step is to determine the WHY. Why do you want to enter network marketing? Is it because you want to be recognised? May be you want to live rich life? A car or a house? Education of your children? You must have a dream to keep you going. Also, ask yourself whether you are ready to commit to it, even if it is one hour a day! In bold, write down your dream.</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="8f1b" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
<span class="it jf" style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: 700;"><em class="jg" style="box-sizing: inherit;">ACTION</em></span>&nbsp;&#8212; WRITE YOUR TOP FIVE DREAMS THAT YOU WISH TO FULFILL WITH THIS BUSINESS.</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="6b5a" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
<span class="it jf" style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: 700;">STEP 2: DECIDE A GOAL.</span></div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="1f3f" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
Each step is going to build a great foundation to your network marketing business. Now that you know why you want to get in network marketing, the second question is how. With help of this guide and your upline (recruiter), decide long term, yearly and monthly goals.</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="adf2" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
For example,</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="9342" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
Long term goal &#8212; earn Rs. 1,00,000 a month by 2019</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="f7fd" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
Yearly goal &#8212; 2017 &#8212; earn my first 30,000</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="0bc5" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
Monthly goal &#8212; recruit and train 5 new leads.</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="1364" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
A goal will help you stay focussed and a plan will tell your brain what is needed to be done to reach that goal.</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="c1a7" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
<span class="it jf" style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: 700;"><em class="jg" style="box-sizing: inherit;">ACTION</em></span>&nbsp;&#8212; WRITE DOWN GOALS ON A PAPER AND DISPLAY IT WHERE YOU CAN SEE IT EVERY SINGLE DAY.</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="c3eb" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
<span class="it jf" style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: 700;">STEP 3: TAKE TRAININGS OFFERED BY YOUR COMPANY AND LEADER.</span></div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="b5a3" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
The company you are working with knows the best way to become successful in that business. Thus, follow their training program and learn various tactics to form a successful customer base. Most of the good MLM companies will have excellent training program. This is where choosing a good leader is worth an effort. If you have a good leader, you will have a person to look upon, a mentor to help you grow, and a friend to celebrate small victories together!</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="faa8" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
<span class="it jf" style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: 700;"><em class="jg" style="box-sizing: inherit;">ACTION</em></span>&nbsp;&#8212; ATTEND COMPANY TRAININGS AND LEARN REQUIRED SKILLS</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="c1c3" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
<span class="it jf" style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: 700;">STEP 4: MAKE A KILLER ACTION PLAN.</span></div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="2736" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
After you have mastered the how-to&#8217;s of business, it&#8217;s time to put goals to action. Based on your dream, goals and trainings, plan your calendar. Create a weekly task list of the month and share it with your Downline/ team members. Remember, in network marketing business, the more your team members grow, the better.</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="d265" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
Your whole action plan must focus on 1.&nbsp;<span class="it jf" style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: 700;">Team members</span>&nbsp;and 2.&nbsp;<span class="it jf" style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: 700;">New personal recruits</span>. You are a leader and you are responsible for the growth of your team.</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="8372" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
ACTION &#8212; CREATE A MONTHLY CALENDER OF TASKS FOR SELF AND TEAM MEMBERS.</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="0d10" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
<span class="it jf" style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: 700;">STEP 5: PLAN TO ACTION.</span></div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="c626" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
Most of the people stop after planning, successful people convert their plans into reality. While 80% will give up after planning, be a leader and put your effort to convert your dream into reality. Your monthly goals should include</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="dae2" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
1. Sales of minimum required order</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="1750" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
2. Recruiting new team members</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="f7e1" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
3. Training existing team members</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="73c8" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
This is all you have to keep in mind. All your campaigns must revolve around these three important goals. Here on this blog, you will find how to achieve success in each of these goals, so stay tuned.</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="4343" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
ACTION &#8212; PUT YOURSELVES OUT THERE AND GENERATE POTENTIAL LEADS, TRAIN TEAM MEMBERS.</div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="2511" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
<span class="it jf" style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: 700;">STEP 6: REVIEW YOUR ACTIONS.</span></div>
<div class="ir is er bg it b iu iv iw ix iy iz ja jb jc jd je" data-selectable-paragraph="" id="ce26" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: inherit; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.84); font-family: medium-content-serif-font, Georgia, Cambria, &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, Times, serif; font-size: 21px; letter-spacing: -0.004em; line-height: 1.58; margin-bottom: -0.46em; margin-top: 2em;">
At the end of each week or month, reflect on what you have achieved, the opportunities you have missed, and what can be improved. Celebrate your victory with team members and learn from the mistakes you made this month.
